## Innovations Published (Prior Art)

### 1. Harm-Asymmetric Decision Gating
A method for applying differentiated confidence thresholds based on harm type:
- Physical safety interventions: 80% confidence + 3-tick hysteresis
- Reputational/amplification actions: 95% confidence + 5-tick hysteresis
- Evidence-gap escalation to 95% when critical information missing

### 2. Multi-AI Coordination Protocol
Architecture enabling heterogeneous AI systems to collaborate with:
- Role-based task assignment (Layer 1-4 architecture)
- Binding constraints preventing single-system dominance
- Transparent conflict resolution with full audit trail
- Independent verification by each participating system

### 3. Counterfactual Shadow Layer
System requirement to maintain strongest alternative hypothesis alongside 
primary hypothesis, with confidence updates discounted unless they actively 
weaken the counterfactual.

### 4. Evidence Gap Detection and Escalation
Automated detection of missing critical evidence (predefined taxonomy) 
with mandatory threshold escalation when gaps identified.

### 5. Mandatory Equal-Visibility Retraction Protocol
Architectural requirement for retractions to receive equal algorithmic 
visibility as original actions, with immutable audit trail.
